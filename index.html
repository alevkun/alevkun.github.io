Aleksandr Levkun

Welcome to my website!

My name is Aleksandr (Sasha) Levkun. I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Economics of the WU Vienna University of Economics and Business. Prior to that, I was a Visiting Assistant Professor at the Bilkent University. I am an economic theorist specializing in Information Economics. My research focuses on the strategic mediation of information.

I received my Ph.D. from the Economics Department at the University of California San Diego in 2022.

You can reach me at alevkun@ucsd.edu.
